Swap two bits controlled on a control bit.

This is sometimes known as the [Fredkin Gate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fredkin_gate).

#### Registers
 - `ctrl`: the control bit
 - `x`: the first bit
 - `y`: the second bit
